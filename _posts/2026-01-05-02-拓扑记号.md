# 记号

$$
    D^n:=\{x\in \mathbb{R}^n:\|x\|\leq 1\}
$$

$$
    S^{n-1}:=\partial D^n = \{x\in \mathbb{R}^n:\|x\|=1\}
$$

$$
    T^n = S^1\times\cdots\times S^1
$$

$$
    \mathbb{R} P^n = \mathbb{R}^{n+1}\setminus\{0\}/\sim
$$

$$
    \mathbb{C} P^n = \mathbb{C}^{n+1}\setminus\{0\}/\sim
$$
