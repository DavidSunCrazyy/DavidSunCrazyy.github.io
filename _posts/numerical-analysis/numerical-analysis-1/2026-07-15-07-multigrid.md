本文介绍多重网格法的基本思想与实现，从一维模型问题出发，通过阻尼 Jacobi 迭代的特征值分析展示高频与低频误差分量的衰减特性，进而引入双重网格法的 V-Cycle 框架，并给出基于离散 Fourier 模的收敛性分析。

# 4.4 多重网格法

## - 模型问题

考虑一维边值问题.

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} - \frac {d ^ {2} u}{d x ^ {2}} = f (x) & x \in \Omega = (0, 1) \\ u (0) = u (1) = 0 \end{array} \right.
$$

将 $\Omega$ 均匀剖分为 N 份，记

$$
\Omega_ {h} = \{x _ {j} = j h, j = 0, 1, 2, \dots , N \}
$$

其中 $h = \frac{1}{N}$

采用中心差分离散可得差分格式：

$$
- \frac {u _ {j + 1} + u _ {j - 1} - 2 u _ {j}}{h ^ {2}} = f (x _ {j}) \quad j = 1, \dots , N - 1
$$

$$
u _ {0} = u _ {N} = 0
$$

写成方程组形式为

$$
\begin{array}{r l} {L _ {h}} & {u = F \qquad (4. 4. 1)} \\ {\mathrm{其中}} & {u = [ u _ {1}, \dots , u _ {N - 1} ] ^ {T} \qquad , \quad F = [ f (x _ {1}) \dots f (x _ {N-1}) ] ^ {T}} \\ {L _ {h}} & {= \frac {1}{h ^ {2}} \left[ \begin{array}{l l l l l} 2 & - 1 & & & \\ - 1 & 2 & - 1 & & \\ & \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & \\ & & - 1 & 2 & - 1 \\ & & & - 1 & 2 \end{array} \right] \in \mathbb {R} ^ {(N - 1) \times (N - 1)}} \end{array}
$$

# - 差分方程组（4.4.1）迭代法分析

采用阻尼Jacobi迭代法求解(4.4.1)

$$
u ^ {(n + 1)} = u ^ {(n)} - \omega D _ {h} ^ {- 1} (L _ {h} u ^ {(n)} - F) \quad (4. 4. 2)
$$

其中 $D_{h} = \operatorname{diag}(L_{h}) = \frac{2}{h^{2}} I$

$0 < \omega \leq 1$ 为可调参数.

将 $L_h$ 的具体形式代入 (4.4.2) 得

$$
u ^ {(n + 1)} = \left[ I - \frac {\omega}{2} (2 I - S) \right] u ^ {(n)} + \frac {\omega}{2} h ^ {2} F
$$

这里

$$
S = \left[ \begin{array}{l l l l} 0 & 1 & & \\ 1 & 0 & 1 & \\ & \ddots & \ddots & \\ & \ddots & \ddots & \\ & 1 & 0 & 1 \\ & & 1 & 0 \end{array} \right]
$$

该迭代法收敛的充要条件为

$$
\rho (J _ {\omega}) <   1,
$$

Jω 为 (4.4.2) 的迭代矩阵.

$$
J _ {\omega} = I - \frac {\omega}{2} (2 I - S)
$$

S 的特征值为 $2 \cos \mu \pi h, \quad \mu = 1, 2, \cdots, N - 1$ 相应的特征向量为 $e^{\mu} = \sqrt{2h} \left[ \sin \mu x h, \sin 2 \mu x h, \cdots, \sin (N - 1) \mu x h \right]^T$ 由此可得 $J_{\omega}$ 的特征值为

$$
\lambda_ {\mu} (\omega) = 1 - 2 \omega \sin^ {2} \frac {\mu h \pi}{2}, \mu = 1, 2, \dots N - 1
$$

容易验证 $e^{\mu}$ 构成了一组单位正交基

设迭代法的初始向量为 $u^{(0)}$ ，u 为 $(4.4.1)$ 的精确解。则初始误差可在 $e^{\mu}$ 上展开

$$
u ^ {(0)} - u = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {N - 1} \alpha_ {k} e ^ {k}
$$

迭代 $n$ 步后，误差变为

$$
\begin{array}{r l} u ^ {(n)} - u & = (J _ {\omega}) ^ {n} (u ^ {(0)} - u) \\ & = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {N - 1} \alpha_ {k} (J _ {\omega}) ^ {n} e ^ {k} \\ & = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {N - 1} \alpha_ {k} (\lambda_ {k} (\omega)) ^ {n} e ^ {k} \end{array}
$$

不同分量的衰减速度取决于相应的特征值.

通常将误差分量分成两组：低频误差分量 $0 < kh \leq \frac{1}{2}$ 和高频误差分量 ( $\frac{1}{2} < kh \leq 1$ )

另一方面，误差分量的高频和低频是相对的细网格上的低频分量在粗网格上就可能变成高频分量，从而可以利用粗网格上的迭代将该误差分量快速消除掉。然后再将解通过插值返回到细网格，这就是多重网格法的基本想法

\- 双重网格法.

在 (4.4.1) 中对 $\Omega$ 引入两套网格

$$
\Omega_ {h} = \{x _ {j} = j h, \quad j = 0, 1, \dots , N \}
$$

$$
\Omega_ {2 h} = \{x _ {j} = 2 j h, j = 0, 1, \dots , \frac {N}{2} \}
$$

则求解 (4.4.1) 的双重网格法描述如下：

(1) 在细网格 $\Omega_{h}$ 上用迭代方法求解

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} (L _ {h} u _ {h}) _ {j} = f _ {h, j}, j = 1, 2, \dots N - 1 \\ u _ {h, 0} = u _ {h, N} = 0 \end{array} \right.
$$

设迭代进行了 $u_i$ 步，相应的近似解记为 $u_h^{(u_i)}$ ，计算相应的残差：

$$
r _ {h, j} = f _ {h, j} - (L _ {h} u _ {h} ^ {(i)}) _ {j}, j = 1, 2, \dots N - 1
$$

则误差 $e_{n,j} = u_{n,j} - u_{n,j}^{(n)}$ 满足.

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} (L _ {h} e _ {h}) _ {j} = r _ {h, j}, & j = 1, 2, \dots , N - 1 \\ e _ {h, 0} = e _ {h, N} = 0 \end{array} \right.
$$

(2) 将细网格上的误差转移到粗网格 $\Omega_{2h}$ 上，记 $I_{h}^{2h}$ 为 $\Omega_{h}$ 到 $\Omega_{2h}$ 的限制算子.
记 $I_{2h}^{h}$ 为 $\Omega_{2h}$ 到 $\Omega_{h}$ 的插值算子

(3) 在 $\Omega_{2h}$ 上求解

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} (L _ {2 h} e _ {2 h}) _ {j} & = (I _ {h} ^ {2 h} r _ {h}) _ {j}, j = 1, 2, \dots , \frac {N}{2} - 1 \\ e _ {2 h, 0} & = e _ {2 h, \frac {N}{2}} = 0 \end{array} \right.
$$

得 ${e}_{2h} = {L}_{2h}^{-1}{r}_{2h}$

其中 $L_{2h}=I_{h}^{2h}L_{h}I_{2h}^{h}$

(4) 将 $e_{2h}$ 插值到 $\Omega_{h}$ 上，记 $I_{2h}^{h}$ 为 $\Omega_{2h}$ 到 $\Omega_{h}$ 的插值算子，则有

$$
\tilde {e} _ {h} = I _ {2 h} ^ {h} e _ {2 h}
$$

(5) 在 $\Omega_{h}$ 上修正近似解

$$
u _ {h} ^ {(\nu_ {i})} = u _ {h} ^ {(\nu_ {i})} + \tilde {e} _ {h}
$$

以修正后的解作为初始解迭代步骤得到新的近似解.

这一过程通常称为 $V - Cycle$

多重网格法．多个 V-cycle 合并可得 W-cycle

常用的插值算子有线性插值，即

$$
\begin{array}{r l} \widetilde {e} _ {h, 2 j - 1} & = \frac {1}{2} (e _ {2 h, j - 1} + e _ {2 h, j}) \\ \widetilde {e} _ {h, 2 j} & = e _ {2 h, j} \quad j = 1, 2, \dots , \frac {N}{2} \end{array}
$$

写成矩阵形式为

$$
I _ {2 h} ^ {h} = \left[ \begin{array}{l l l l l} 1 & & & & \\ \frac {1}{2} & \frac {1}{2} & & & \\ & 1 & & & \\ & \frac {1}{2} & \frac {1}{2} & & \\ & & \ddots & & \\ & & & \frac {1}{2} & \frac {1}{2} \\ & & & & 1 \end{array} \right] \in \mathbb {R} ^ {(N + 1) \times (\frac {N}{2} + 1)}
$$

限制算子 一般取作 插值算子的 对偶算子

即

$$
I _ {h} ^ {2 h} = \left(I _ {2 h} ^ {h}\right) ^ {T}
$$

\- 收敛性分析

一次 双重网格迭代的过程可以总结为

$$
x ^ {(n + 1)} = \tilde {x} ^ {(n + 1)} + I _ {2 h} ^ {h} (L _ {2 h}) ^ {- 1} I _ {h} ^ {2 h} (f _ {h} - L _ {h} \tilde {x} ^ {(n + 1)})
$$

其中 $\widetilde{x}^{(n+1)}$ 为细网格 $\Omega_{h}$ 上进行若干步迭代得

到的解

另一方面 精确解 满足

$$
x ^ {*} = x ^ {*} + I _ {2 h} ^ {h} (L _ {2 h}) ^ {- 1} I _ {h} ^ {2 h} (f _ {h} - L _ {h} x ^ {*})
$$

两式相减得

$$
e ^ {(n + 1)} = e ^ {\sim (n + 1)} - I _ {2 h} ^ {h} (L _ {2 h}) ^ {- 1} I _ {h} ^ {2 h} L _ {h} e ^ {\sim (n + 1)}
$$

注意到

$$
\tilde{e} ^ {(n + 1)} = B ^ {\alpha} e ^ {(n)}
$$

其中 B 为细网格上的迭代矩阵， $\alpha$ 为迭代次数

$$
\begin{array}{r l} {e ^ {(n + 1)}} & {= B ^ {\alpha} e ^ {(n)} - I _ {2 h} ^ {h} (L _ {2 h}) ^ {- 1} I _ {h} ^ {2 h} L _ {h} B ^ {\alpha} e ^ {(n)}} \\ & {= (I - I _ {2 h} ^ {h} (L _ {2 h}) ^ {- 1} I _ {h} ^ {2 h} L _ {h}) B ^ {\alpha} e ^ {(n)}} \\ & {= B _ {M G} e ^ {(n)}} \end{array}
$$

$$
\mathrm{令} W _ {k} ^ {h} = [ \sin (j k \pi h) ], j. k = 1, \dots , N - 1
$$

则有

$$
\begin{array}{r l} {\left(L _ {h} W _ {k} ^ {h}\right) _ {j}} & = \frac {1}{h ^ {2}} \left(\sin (j + 1) k \pi h - 2 \sin j k \pi h + \sin (j - 1) k \pi h\right) \\ & = \frac {1}{h ^ {2}} \left(2 \cos k \pi h - 2\right) \sin j k \pi h \\ & = - \frac {4}{h ^ {2}} \sin^ {2} \frac {k \pi}{2 N} \quad \sin j k \pi h \\ & = - \frac {4}{h ^ {2}} \sin^ {2} \frac {k \pi}{2 N} \quad (W _ {k} ^ {h}) _ {j} \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{r l} {\left(I _ {h} ^ {2 h} w _ {k} ^ {h}\right) _ {j}} & = {\frac {1}{2} \sin (2 j + 1) k \pi h + \sin 2 j k \pi h + \frac {1}{2} \sin (2 j - 1) k \pi h} \\ & = {(1 + \cos k \pi h) \sin 2 j k \pi h} \\ & = {2 \cos^ {2} (\frac {k \pi}{2 N}) \left(W _ {k} ^ {2 h}\right) _ {j}, \quad k = 1, \dots , \frac {N}{2} - 1} \end{array}
$$

$$
\left(I _ {h} ^ {2 h} W _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h}\right) _ {j} = - 2 \sin^ {2} \left(\frac {k \pi}{2 N}\right) \left(W _ {k} ^ {2 h}\right) _ {j}, \quad k = N - k ^ {\prime} \quad k ^ {\prime} = \frac {N}{2} + 1 \dots N - 1
$$

$$
(I _ {h} ^ {2 h} W _ {N / 2} ^ {h}) _ {j} = 0
$$

$$
\left(I _ {2 h} ^ {h} W _ {k} ^ {2 h}\right) _ {j} = \sin j k \pi h, \quad j \text {为偶数}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{r l r} {(I _ {2 h} ^ {h} w _ {k} ^ {2 h}) _ {j}} & = & {\frac {1}{2} \left(\sin (\frac {j - 1}{2}) k \pi 2 h + \sin (\frac {j + 1}{2}) k \pi 2 h\right)} \\ & = & {\cos k \pi h \sin j k \pi h, j \mathrm{为奇数}} \end{array}
$$

$$
I _ {2 h} ^ {h} w _ {k} ^ {2 h} = \cos^ {2} \left(\frac {k \pi}{2 N}\right) w _ {k} ^ {h} - \sin^ {2} \left(\frac {k \pi}{2 N}\right) w _ {k} ^ {h},
$$

$$
k ^ {\prime} = N - k
$$

$$
\mathrm{令} C _ {k} = \cos^ {2} (\frac {k \pi}{2 N}), S _ {k} = \sin^ {2} (\frac {k \pi}{2 N})
$$

$$
\begin{array}{r l} {L _ {2 h} w _ {k} ^ {2 h}} & {= I _ {h} ^ {2 h} L _ {h} I _ {2 h} ^ {h} w _ {k} ^ {2 h}} \\ & {= I _ {h} ^ {2 h} L _ {h} (C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {h} - S _ {k} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h})} \\ & {= - \frac {4}{h ^ {2}} I _ {h} ^ {2 h} (S _ {k} C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {h} - S _ {k} C _ {k} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h})} \\ & {= - \frac {4}{h ^ {2}} S _ {k} C _ {k} (2 C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {2 h} + 2 S _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {2 h})} \\ & {= - \frac {8}{h ^ {2}} S _ {k} C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {2 h}} \end{array}
$$

总结以上计算得

$$
L _ {h} w _ {k} ^ {h} = - \frac {4}{h ^ {2}} S _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {h}, L _ {h} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h} = - \frac {4}{h ^ {2}} C _ {k} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h}
$$

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} I _ {h} ^ {2 h} w _ {k} ^ {h} & = 2 C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {2 h}, k = 1 \dots \frac {N}{2} - 1 \\ I _ {h} ^ {2 h} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h} & = - 2 S _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {2 h}, k ^ {\prime} = N - k \\ I _ {h} ^ {2 h} w _ {N / 2} ^ {h} & = 0 \end{array} \right.
$$

$$
\begin{array}{r c l} I _ {2 h} ^ {h} w _ {k} ^ {2 h} & = & C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {h} - S _ {k} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h}, \quad k ^ {\prime} = N - k \\ L _ {2 h} w _ {k} ^ {2 h} & = & - \frac {8}{h ^ {2}} S _ {k} C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {2 h} \end{array}
$$

设 $B{w}_{k}^{h} = {\lambda }_{k}{w}_{k}^{h}$

则有

$$
\begin{array}{r l} {B _ {m G} w _ {k} ^ {h}} & {= (I - I _ {2 h} ^ {h} (L _ {2 h}) ^ {- 1} I _ {h} ^ {2 h} L _ {h}) B ^ {\alpha} w _ {k} ^ {h}} \\ & {= (w _ {k} ^ {h} - C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {h} + S _ {k} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h}) \lambda_ {k} ^ {\alpha}} \\ & {= (w _ {k} ^ {h} + w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h}) S _ {k} \lambda_ {k} ^ {\alpha}, \quad k = 1 \dots \frac {N}{2} - 1} \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{r l} {B _ {M G} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h}} & {= (w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h} + C _ {k} w _ {k} ^ {h} - S _ {k} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h})} \\ & {= (w _ {k} ^ {h} + w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h}) C _ {k} \lambda_ {k} ^ {\alpha}, \quad k ^ {\prime} = N - k} \end{array}
$$

$$
B _ {M G} w _ {N / 2} ^ {h} = \lambda_ {N / 2} ^ {\alpha} w _ {N / 2} ^ {h}
$$

设

$$
e ^ {(n)} = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {N / 2} \beta_ {k} w _ {k} ^ {h} + \sum_ {k ^ {\prime} = \frac {N}{2} + 1} ^ {N - 1} \beta_ {k ^ {\prime}} w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h}
$$

则有

$$
\begin{array}{r l} {e ^ {(n + 1)}} & {= B _ {M G} e ^ {(n)}} \\ & {= \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {N / 2} S _ {k} \lambda_ {k} ^ {\alpha} \beta_ {k} (w _ {k} ^ {h} + w _ {N - k} ^ {h})} \\ & {+ \sum_ {k ^ {\prime} = \frac {N}{2} + 1} ^ {N - 1} C _ {k} \lambda_ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {\alpha} \beta_ {k ^ {\prime}} (w _ {N - k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h} + w _ {k ^ {\prime}} ^ {h})} \\ & {= \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {\frac {N}{2} - 1} (S _ {k} \lambda_ {k} ^ {\alpha} \beta_ {k} + C _ {k} \lambda_ {N - k} ^ {\alpha} \beta_ {N - k}) (w _ {k} ^ {h} + w _ {N - k} ^ {h})} \\ & {+ \lambda_ {N / 2} ^ {\alpha} \beta_ {N / 2} w _ {N / 2} ^ {h}} \end{array}
$$
